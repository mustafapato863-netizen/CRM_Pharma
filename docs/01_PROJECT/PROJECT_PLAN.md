# Project Plan

## Purpose

Tracks the product vision, scope, milestones, and delivery strategy for the simplified internal Pharmacy CRM.

## Responsibilities

- Define the business goals and target users.
- Summarize the selected technology stack.
- Link to architecture and database documents.
- Describe delivery phases without duplicating implementation details.

## What Should Be Documented Here

- Project overview.
- Goals and non-goals.
- Stakeholders.
- High-level feature list.
- Delivery phases.
- Production readiness criteria.

## MVP Summary

- Internal use only.
- 2 to 3 users.
- Stock movements and balances only.
- Parties instead of separate customers and suppliers.
- Ledger instead of accounting.
- No invoices, warehouses, or enterprise features.
