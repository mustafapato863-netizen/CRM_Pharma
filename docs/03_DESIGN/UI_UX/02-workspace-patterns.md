# Workspace Patterns

## Dashboard Workspace

Purpose:
- Show business health at a glance
- Give quick navigation into the next action

Layout:
- KPI cards first
- Alerts second
- Quick actions third
- Detailed analytics last

## Inventory Workspace

Purpose:
- Move stock quickly
- Reduce friction in stock entry and review

Layout:
- Movement summary
- Fast entry panel
- Table / movement history
- Recent movement side info

## Purchase Workspace

Purpose:
- Create invoices fast
- Receive stock with minimum clicks

Layout:
- Supplier selection
- Invoice body
- Batch creation / selection
- Summary and confirmation

## Sales Workspace

Purpose:
- Issue invoices fast
- Make FEFO selection obvious

Layout:
- Customer selection
- Product selection
- Batch selection
- Review and submit

## Ledger Workspace

Purpose:
- Review balances quickly
- Move between accounts without confusion

Layout:
- Account summary
- Filter bar
- Transaction timeline or table
- Balance footer

## Reports Workspace

Purpose:
- Analyze data quickly
- Export without hunting controls

Layout:
- Report cards
- Primary filters
- Results area
- Export / print actions

## Settings Workspace

Purpose:
- Configure the system with minimal friction

Layout:
- Sections in cards
- Clean form controls
- Clear save/reset actions

## Party Workspace

Purpose:
- Manage customers and suppliers from one place

Layout:
- Summary cards
- Filter row
- Table
- Drawer-based create/edit flow
